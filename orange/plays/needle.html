2 weeks before the regional exams, me and my fellow friends recorded the play which is going to be performed infront of 300 kids. Outside was misty - an indicator that the school year is about to end. the Enviroment was cold - but perfet for us.
We took the whole thing in three takes. I had made a stand using a water bottle and a nut and fixed the camera on top of it. It gave me more control of the camera, and alleviated the problem whether my hands will cover the camera's lens since it was small.
I edited it all on an iPhone using an app called Capcut. I had no idea about video editing, but the simple and easy-to-use interface of the app got me to know the basics in no time. I finished the video that day - and sent it to a telegram group.

Here's some photos we took that day and 2 days before... Enjoy.
